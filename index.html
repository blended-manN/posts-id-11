Follicle Realm: Worldbuilding Log

Current State of the Follicle Realm as a Project

The Follicle Realm, as a project, has been largely reset since I've last worked on it actively last year. This includes Proto-Folliccanese, which I've changed the grammar on. You can view information on the language here: Proto-Folliccanese Info . As you can see, I've restructured the map a bit. All the names on the map are working names, these will not be the final names of the regions. Those will come from the multiple languages are plan to make, or at least I will name them in accordance with one language. The area that will be of the most focus of future Follicle media and stories will be the area that compromises Gelderland and the southern half of Volunatchvadhloudhe (the central continent of the world). This area is the extent to which Folliccanese cultures (here refering to the group of cultures that have their origins in the Follicle Fjords, not refering to all Follicles or Folliccites) have spread. It should be noted that the Realm is a flat disk with a dome containing the heavens (thus the forest nearest to the edge of the world, and therefore closest to the dome, is called the Great Forest of the Stars)

These are the biomes I have marked in so far. Brown is tundra, Orange is cold desert, Darkest Green is temperate forest, Second Darkest Green is tropical rainforest, The Shade of Green along the Pink Coast is temperate rainforest, the Shade of Green comprising most of Trym is prairie and grassland, the zone of transition between them is shrubland, grey represents various mountain and highland biomes.

Initial sketch of the Follicle Realm. I realized it looked like Earth with wonky shapes so I removed the "North" and "South" landmasses (the cardinal directions in the realm are sunward and rimward, as all compasses point towards the Sun above the center of the world. Radial direction is usually measured in degrees relative to some important site on the rim)
